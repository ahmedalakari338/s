Our Destinations

3 different countries to choose from.

Australia

From the tropical rain forests in the north, to the temperate mountain ranges in the south and the alpine areas of New South Wales and Victoria there is plenty of things to see and diverse terrian to choose from!

New Zealand

New Zealand the land of boiling mud, is so diverse, with live volcanos, hot springs, snow covered peaks in the winter, hiking in New Zealand is an experience not to miss and one that should be on your must do list!

Thailand

Thailand, known the world over as the Land of Smiles, with it's enique cuisine and ancient ruins, is another place you must visit, our trekking tours in the north in the Chiang Mai and Chiang Rai areas are fantastic!
